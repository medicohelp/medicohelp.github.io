Medical Entrance & Job Exams

A concise overview of major medical entrance and recruitment exams in India.

NEET UG

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) is the single entrance examination for admission to MBBS and BDS courses across India.

  • Conducted by: NTA
  • Frequency: Once a year
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB
Official Website →

NEET PG

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Postgraduate) is required for admission into MD/MS and PG Diploma courses in India.

  • Conducted by: NBE
  • Frequency: Once a year
  • Eligibility: MBBS degree
Official Website →

INI-CET

Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test for PG admissions in AIIMS, JIPMER, PGI Chandigarh and other INIs.

  • Conducted by: AIIMS Delhi
  • Frequency: Twice a year
  • Eligibility: MBBS degree
Official Website →

State & Job Exams

Medical Officer, Senior Resident and faculty recruitment exams conducted by state governments and institutions.
